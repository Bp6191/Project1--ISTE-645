Misleading / Fake NEWS

Misinformation / Disinformation

Propaganda — This refers to false or distorted stories written to mislead audiences and promote a political agenda or biased perspective.

Misinformation, disinformation, and fake news, though not new phenomena, have received renewed scholarly interest following political events such as Brexit and the 2016 U.S. Presidential election. The surge in academic publications has introduced risks of fragmentation across disciplines and a lack of research cumulativity. Since these developments, research on misinformation, disinformation, and fake news has grown substantially (Arcob. A, O, 2023).

Website Summary

This website, "Fact Check HUB," is dedicated to providing accurate and timely information on popular topics and fact-checking recent events, especially around misinformation. Key sections include "Our Fact Checks on Things President-Elect Trump Allegedly Said During 2024 Campaign," which features a dedicated page that users can access by clicking on the main page article link. The website also includes secondary pages for important sections such as About, Login/Signup, and Contact to enhance user navigation and engagement. In addition, the website is designed with a modern, clean layout and responsive design, making it accessible and easy to use across devices.

Website Synopsis

Strengths: Three factors that were done particularly well include the responsive design, which ensures the website looks good on various devices; the navigation, which is clear and easy to use; and the secondary pages, which provide additional information and improve organization. These elements contribute to an overall polished and professional user experience.

Areas for Improvement: Two areas that could use improvement are the styling of the footer to better align with the rest of the design, and the page load speed, which could be enhanced by optimizing images and media files. However, there are two areas that need improvement. First, the styling of the footer could be refined to better match the rest of the design for a cohesive appearance. Second, the page load speed needs optimization, particularly by compressing images and media files to improve performance.

Extras Included

Several "Extras" have been incorporated into the website. One of the primary extras is the use of a JavaScript-powered search bar that allows users to search for specific articles and topics, enhancing navigation. Additionally, a video element was embedded on one of the pages to support the content, providing users with a more immersive and interactive experience.

To find these extras, navigate to the homepage and use the search bar at the top of the page. The video element can be found in the "Our Fact Checks on Things President-Elect Trump Allegedly Said During 2024 Campaign" article, adding valuable visual content to support the topic.

In addition to these, a form validation feature was implemented on the Contact page. When users attempt to submit the form without filling in the required fields, an indicator prompts them to complete the missing information. This ensures a smoother user interaction and eliminates submission errors.
